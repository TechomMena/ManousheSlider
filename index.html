How To Use Our Natural & Organic Dough Slider

Here's a simple guide on how to use our best dough slider

1-Placement
Position the slider next to the object or surface where you want to apply it.
2-Pull Gently
Pull the slider gently to extend the dough or material attached to it.
3-Push to Desired Location
Once you've extended the dough , push the slider along with the dough to the location where you want to place it. This allows you to transfer the dough smoothly and accurately to the desired spot.
